History Of

VIDEO BOYS

Since its establishment in 1985 by K.V. Ganeshan, Video Boys has become a trusted name in photography and videography. Today, Prakash and Sangeetha, family members of the founder, proudly carry on the legacy. With a skilled team of photographers and videographers, Video Boys has honed their craft, specializing in weddings, corporate events, and school functions. Their dedication to excellence and cutting-edge technology sets them apart. Combining creativity and professionalism, Video Boys captures the essence of every occasion, delivering captivating visuals and unforgettable memories. Trust Video Boys for your next event and witness their expertise in capturing life's precious moments with artistic flair.
